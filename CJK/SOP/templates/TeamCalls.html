Team Calls

Creating Team Calls

Team Call schedule: Every Tuesday and Friday

Time: 10 AM

Tools: Google Calender

Notes

For creating team calls, notification should be set to 20 minutes before the team call.

Invitation for team call will not be sent through any communication channel. Its Attendees responsiblities to follow their schedule and attend the team calls regularly without fail.

The event organiser should take notes during the team calls.

Scheduling Interviews

Notes

When creating interview calls in google calendar, acknowledge to the correspondind person in the communiction channel after creating the event.

Interview call timings will be fixed by the corresponding member.

Invite both interviewer and interviewee Email Id in the event.

For creating Interview calls, notification should be set for 20 minutes before.

For calls with different timezone, always set to IST.

If there is any changes in appointments or if cancelled, always notifiy the changes through mail to the corresponding memebers.

Notes for Attendees

Before creating event or a team call get feedback from corresponding members whether they will be available for the team calls or Events.

If any members need to cancel or postpone the team call or event, its mandatory to notify all the members about the changes.
